Time Constant Challenge

     

        Drag the window onto the coordinates for the time constant τ. Releasing your mouse button makes a guess. The curve can either be an exponential decay or an exponential rise.
        Decay: i(t) = i₀·e−t/τ
        Rise: i(t) = i₀·(1 - e−t/τ)
